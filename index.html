Welcome to the chubb club! 

Be part of this 3D NFT project featuring charismatic art and rewarding benefits. 

Or don't. No scales off my back. 


Chubby Gator was born on a particularly muggy evening on the outskirts of the Florida Everglades swamp, underneath the dock of a restaurant known locally for its all-you-can-eat gator-tail special, and nestled in a warm patch of mud and irony.


Their father was a Drunky Gator who split soon after learning of the pregnancy, and their mother came from a long line of Plumpy Gators, genetically predisposed to having big appetites and slow metabolisms.


Growing up, Chubby Gator slowly started to feel like they were different from the rest of the alligators.Chubby Gator wasn't satisfied with the monotonous swamp life, always eating the same old creatures, drinking the same old swamp water; nothing seemed to adequately satiate them, and they didn't feel like their true self. Little by little, the mysterious sounds and alluring smells of the nearby restaurant aroused their interest and appetite to such a crescendo that they decided to venture out beyond the stagnant swale that they were born into and discover the tastes of the world.


Now, you may ask, why is our reptilian so ravishingly round? Why so playfully plump, borderline irreverently rotund? Well, in addition to some tendencies inherited from mom and dad, Chubby Gator also developed a kind of personality where they do what they want, when they want, and portion control is not on the to-do list. After watching their mother eat most of their siblings upon hatching, Chubby Gator figured that nothing is off limits when it comes to indulging oneself.


These days, our chubby friend is a relatively well-adjusted alligator who follows whatever piques their curiosity, regardless of what the rules or norms are, or whatever laws of space and time there might be in the way. This often means following their appetite into random situations, traveling to different places, and having new experiences.


The Chubby Gator is not ashamed of their belly, rather they flaunt their flab; they own it, they wear it as a pudgy plate of armor against anyone who might criticize.They are heavy but like to keep things light.They will be the first one to make fun of themself, before pointing their barbs at the rest of the world.They love socializing with genuine people, and they walk this earth with a charmingly brave naivete that makes people gravitate towards them.


Can't wait to see what they do next!

In a Nutshell


Chubby Gators as a creative collective:

Chubby Gators is a multi-cultural, multi-national, mixed-gender team of artists, engineers, university teachers, and entrepreneurs whose common thread is a love of technology, creativity, and levity.


The Chubby Gators collection: 

A collection of tubby-tastic digital art pieces featuring our charismatic and corpulent gator as they find themself in all types of comical situations and outfits, usually with their next meal or drink on the mind.  The collection will be released in much-anticipated installments, just like your favorite TV series and card collections do.   


What we do 

We are creating special 3D digital art collectibles. Each Chubby Gators art collectible is 100% hand made by our team of creators, from idea generation and modeling, to texturing and lighting, to final rendering and compositing. These are not your standard 2D trading card collectibles whose value is based purely on outlandish metaverse promises and arbitrarily determined rarity.  Our pieces are intended to make whoever views them smile, chuckle, giggle, maybe even guffaw once or twice at the really good ones.  Each one takes considerable time and expertise to make, yet we hope the end result reminds us not to take life too seriously. 


What we believe

Inclusiveness is important to us, both in terms of respecting everyone as fellow humans, but also that anyone and everything is fair game as fodder for soliciting a grin.  There are no intended inflammatory messages or subtexts in the pieces, we just want to keep things light.   


There are a lot of collections out there that are generated by algorithms that randomize predetermined sets of properties and characteristics and then spit out thousands of basic trading cards.  Don't get me wrong, there is a beauty in a flawlessly executed mathematical procedure - this is an art in itself for those who appreciate it.  However, we believe that the human element should stand out at the core, and the technology be a vehicle to provide new pathways to enhance and display creativity.  People remain at center-stage here, and we want to remind you that the imperfect human element in a piece of art is a key component of quality. 


The long-term 

Bar Crawling 

We have goals, and having the best social bar in the meat-a-verse is one of them - we will be there, playing games and enjoying digital hedonism with all our Chubby Chosen Ones - but we will also enjoy the journey there and beyond.  


Agility and Flexibility

This is a small team for now but one with a variety of skills and depth of connections and resources. This makes it easy to quickly pivot and grow in whatever direction we need to or want to depending on what the ever-changing environment gives us and what our community / brand ambassadors are into.


Helping animals in need

Chubby Gators cares about its animal brethren. As much as we love indulging ourselves on food and libations, it is more enjoyable when everyone can partake. That is why we are committed to giving back to animal shelters and animal rescues. Since there is a lot of mismanagement out there when it comes to charities and donations, we are currently searching and vetting out the most deserving so we know that the money has a direct and meaningful impact.   

What are the steps to buying a Chubby Gators 3D NFT?

1st Step: Download and install MetaMask

Start at the MetaMask website https://metamask.io where you’ll find instructions on how to download the wallet. Metamask is a cryptocurrency wallet that allows you to store and purchase Chubby Gators 3D NFTs. Metamask can be used as browser extension and as an app via an Apple or Android device.

2nd Step: Create an account and wallet

Start with the “Create a Wallet” option. If you have an existing software wallet and want to transition over to MetaMask, select the “Import wallet” option.

3rd Step: Add funds

Add tokens to your MetaMask wallet to purchase your Chubby Gators collectible. Sending from an exchange (Binance, Coinbase, etc.) will be the most direct option for you to transfer tokens into MetaMask. ETH is the default token, as MetaMask is an Ethereum wallet. 

4th step: Buy Chubby Gators

Once you’ve set up and verified your account, familiarized yourself with it, and added funds to your wallet, you can connect your Metamask to https://opensea.io and your Chubby Gators NFTs! To avoid any issue during the purchasing process, please add 0.05 to 0.08 ETH to cover gas fees. Gas fees are the fees paid to miners for validating a transaction on the Ethereum network.  They vary  according to the level of congestion on the Ethereum blockchain at any given moment.
